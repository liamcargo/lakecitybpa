Tics typically show up around the ages 2-15 years old, with the average being around 6 years old. Boys are typically more common to have developed tourette's syndrome according to Mayo Clinic. Three to Four times more commonin boys. There is no cure for tourette syndrome howver treatments are available. Many people who have tourette syndrome don't need treatment for symptoms often arent troublesome and lessen and become more controllable throughout the teenage years. Mostly throughout their high school life and/or career.
